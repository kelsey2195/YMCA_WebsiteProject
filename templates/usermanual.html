YMCA Companion User Manual

Register Account/Login (User):

- Navigate to the top-right of the home screen and click on “Login” - Type your credentials into the boxes labeled “Username” and “Password” - Click “Login” - If there was a typing error, try again - If an account does not exist, click on “New User” - Enter your desired credentials - Choose whether you would like to be a member or not - Click “Register”

Register for Program (User):

- Navigate to the “Program Search” page - Click “Register” on a Program

Unregister from Program (User):

- Navigate to the “Program Search” page - Click “Unregister” on a Program
